Pioneer Pantry

Pioneer Pantry strives to alleviate food insecurity by providing supplemental food free to those in need within the Point Park University community. We work to change the perception of hunger on campus and provide resources to educate the community about nutrition and healthy eating. The Pioneer Pantry is affiliated with the Greater Pittsburgh Community Food Bank.
